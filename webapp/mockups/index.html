Index page linking to mockups for Harry Potter wiki

This page links to the mockups of our wireframe of Harry Potter wiki.

  • All of the files are in the link to Raquel's git repo

Here are the mockups.

  • Mockup1: This is the home webpage of Harry Potter wiki
  • Mockup2: This is the characters page you will go if you want to see all the characters or certain characters of your choice
  • Mockup3: This is the detail page of a certain character/spell/potion

Some tips navigating through the mockups

There are certain way for you to mock the user experience in g=navigating through three different webpages.

  • You can click on the all caharcters button in mockup1 to jump to mockup2 that shows the characters
  • You can click on the Albus Dumbledore button in mockup2 to jump to mockup3 that gives you the information about our best headmaster and wizard of all times
  • You can click the HP wiki button in mockup2 and mockup3 to jump back to homepage(mockup1)
